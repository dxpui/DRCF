The Digital Regulation Cooperation Forum (DRCF) brings together four UK regulators to deliver a coherent approach to digital regulation for the benefit of people and businesses online.

Working together as the DRCF enables our four member regulators to achieve better outcomes for industry and consumers. The DRCF creates coherence to resolve potential tensions between our members’ regulatory regimes; collaboration to work together on complex challenges; and capabilities for the future, with the right people, skills and critical functions to navigate the rapidly developing digital economy.

Kate Jones, DRCF Chief Executive
