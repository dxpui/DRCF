DRCF Workplan 2024/25
The fourth DRCF Workplan sets out our three-year vision and key areas of focus for 2024/25. We have also published a response to the key themes raised in the Call for Input which we carried out between November and December 2023. You can read a Welsh translation of both documents here.

DRCF Workplan 2023/24
The third DRCF workplan sets out our key areas of focus for 2023/24. To help us identify these, we issued a Call for Input. The responses we received are set out in alphabetical order by stakeholder - (A-C, D-N, O-Y).

DRCF Workplan 2022/23
The DRCF’s second workplan outlined the DRCF’s key workstreams to tackle significant digital challenges, including protecting children online; promoting competition and privacy in online advertising; supporting improvements in algorithmic transparency and enabling innovation in the industries we regulate.
